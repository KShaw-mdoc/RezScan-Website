RezScan Modules

The RezScan platform is composed of focused modules that work together in secure environments.

SessionGuard Concurrent Logons

Monitors concurrent logins, flags suspicious sessions, and provides a dashboard for forensic review and cleanup.

NetScan AI Network Profiling

Discovers devices on the network, profiles risk, and highlights unknown or misconfigured endpoints.

RezOps Viewer Remote Observability

Viewer-focused tooling for observing session activity and supporting investigations with minimal friction.

Intent2Policy Policy Builder

A visual, puzzle-based GPO & intent-to-policy designer that turns real-world rules into enforceable configuration.

MDOC Audit Suite Forensic Tooling

PowerShell and Python-based tooling for browser history, policy alignment, and forensic audit support.
