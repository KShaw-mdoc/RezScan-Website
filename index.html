RezScan™ Technologies is a next-generation platform engineered specifically for secure correctional environments. Designed from inside the system, it focuses on strengthening security, modernizing digital oversight, and supporting controlled access to technology in ways that align with real-world facility operations.

Built for Corrections. By Corrections. RezScan is being developed by a corrections professional who understands the balance between safety, control, governance, and responsible modernization for incarcerated individuals.

High-Level Focus Areas

RezScan is being designed around four core domains that matter most in secure facilities. Specific capabilities, tools, and implementation details are intentionally not listed here for operational security.

Security Safety First

Built with a security-first mindset, RezScan prioritizes controlled access, integrity of systems, and alignment with facility safety requirements.

Audits & Digital Oversight Accountability

Designed to support clearer digital trails and more effective review, helping staff understand what is happening on approved systems without overwhelming complexity.

Network Intelligence Visibility

Focused on improving visibility into approved technology environments so facilities can better understand how systems, devices, and connections are behaving over time.

Secure Education Support Opportunity

Aims to support modern learning experiences in ways that respect security boundaries, giving incarcerated individuals access to structured, supervised technology where it can safely be offered.
